SEOBAIKE Global Scan — AI Market Overview & Industry Outlook 2026-2030
Scan Date: 2026-02-18
Source: CEO Research Compilation
========================================================

=== 1. MARKET SIZE ===
  2023: ~$190B (USD)
  2030 Forecast: ~$1.8T (USD)
  CAGR: 30-36%
  GenAI + Enterprise Automation = primary growth drivers

=== 2. ACTIVE SEGMENTS (Most Active AI Company Types) ===
  A. Foundation Models / Cloud Giants
     OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Meta
     Control: compute, models, platforms (upstream)

  B. Enterprise AI / Automation Platforms
     UiPath, Automation Anywhere, C3.ai, DataRobot
     Focus: embedding AI into enterprise workflows

  C. Vertical AI (Domain-Specific)
     Healthcare, Finance, Legal, Customer Service, Education, Manufacturing
     Take general models → industry-specific solutions
     Currently receiving most VC funding & customer adoption

  D. Physical / Embodied AI
     Robotics, autonomous vehicles, warehouse logistics, factory automation
     Humanoid robots, factory robots, physical agents
     Viewed as "next wave" growth frontier

=== 3. FUTURE OUTLOOK 2026-2030 ===
  Market: Multi-trillion dollar by 2030, 30-35% CAGR
  Business Model Evolution:
    - Tool → Platform → Ecosystem (single tools will be disrupted)
    - One-time project → Long-term subscription + revenue share
    - Outcome-based pricing (cost savings / revenue share)
  Tech Direction:
    - Single model → Composite systems + Agentic AI
    - Multi-model, multi-tool orchestration for complete workflows
    - Physical AI & robotics as next major investment wave
  Risks:
    - Regulation, privacy, cybersecurity, ethics (especially finance/healthcare/gov)
    - Talent gap: "AI-in-process" system designers still scarce

=== 4. WHAT AN AI COMPANY NEEDS TO SURVIVE ===
  Product:
    - Clear product packaging: defined customer, I/O, measurable ROI
  Engineering & Data:
    - MLOps/DataOps teams: deploy, monitor, update models
    - Security & Privacy: GDPR, data law compliance, encryption, permissions
  Business & Domain:
    - Deep vertical industry expertise (healthcare/legal/manufacturing)
    - Customer Success: ongoing adjustment, not just license sales
  Governance & Risk:
    - AI governance team
    - Liability, copyright, data source management

=== 5. VALUATION BENCHMARKS ===
  Legal/Contract/Compliance/Developer Tools/Agentic AI:
    Revenue multiples: 30-50x (vs typical SaaS: 10-20x)
